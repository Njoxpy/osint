# OSINT Tools and Techniques

## Overview

This document outlines essential OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) tools and techniques for information gathering and verification.

## Email Address Discovery

### Primary Email Discovery Tools

- **[Hunter.io](https://hunter.io/)** - Professional email finder and verifier
  - Features department-specific email search functionality
  - Provides email verification and deliverability scoring
  - Offers bulk email discovery for organizations

- **[Phonebook.cz](https://phonebook.cz/)** - Comprehensive contact information database
  - Alternative: [IntelX](https://intelx.io/) for broader search capabilities

- **[VoilaNorbert](https://www.voilanorbert.com/)** - Email finder with high accuracy rates
  - Specializes in finding professional email addresses
  - Includes email verification features

- **[Clearbit Connect](https://clearbit.com/connect)** - Email discovery and enrichment
  - Integrates with Gmail and other email clients
  - Provides additional contact information and company data

- **[VerifyMail.io](https://verifymail.io/)** - Email address verification service
  - Validates email addresses for deliverability
  - Helps reduce bounce rates and improve email campaigns

## Email Discovery Techniques

### Google-Based Methods

1. **Search Engine Queries**
   - Use advanced Google search operators to find email addresses
   - Combine domain names with common email patterns
   - Search for email signatures in documents and web pages

2. **Password Recovery Method**
   - Utilize "Forgot Password" functionality on websites
   - Many sites reveal partial email addresses during recovery process
   - Helps confirm email address patterns for organizations

### Best Practices

- Always verify discovered email addresses before use
- Respect privacy laws and regulations (GDPR, CAN-SPAM, etc.)
- Use multiple tools to cross-verify results
- Document your findings for future reference
